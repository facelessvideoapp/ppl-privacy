01

Data We Collect

We collect absolutely nothing. Parental Lock operates 100% offline and does not transmit any data to any server, company, or third party — ever.

Parental Lock is built on a single principle: your family's information is yours alone. There is no user account, no registration, no email, no phone number, and no cloud storage of any kind. The app functions entirely within your device's local storage.

02

Biometric Data

Parental Lock uses Android's built-in Biometric API to verify the parent's identity for unlocking. This means:

  • Your fingerprint or face data is processed entirely by your phone's secure hardware enclave
  • Parental Lock never sees, reads, stores, or transmits your biometric data
  • The app only receives a simple success or failure result from Android
  • Your biometric data never leaves your device under any circumstances

This is identical to how any other app that uses Android's fingerprint or face unlock works — the biometric itself is always handled by the OS, never by the app.

03

Local Storage

The following minimal data is stored locally on your device only and is never sent anywhere:

  • Timer duration preference (e.g. 30 minutes)
  • Backup PIN (stored securely in Android SharedPreferences)
  • App setup completion status
  • Battery optimization confirmation flag
  • Usage session history (start time, end time, duration) — stored locally for the Usage Tracker screen only

All of this data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app. There is no backup copy anywhere.

04

Third Party Services

Parental Lock does not integrate with any third party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. Specifically, we do not use:

  • Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics
  • Facebook SDK or any Meta services
  • Crashlytics or any crash reporting
  • Any advertising networks or SDKs
  • Any data brokers or marketing platforms

The app does not make any external network connections. All functionality works entirely offline on your device.

05

Children's Privacy

Parental Lock is a tool operated by parents or guardians and installed on a child's device. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults.

We are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and all applicable regional children's privacy laws. Since we collect no data at all, there is no data to protect, share, or delete.

No data is ever collected from children. The app functions as a local tool only — it locks the device and keeps a local usage log visible only to the parent on that device.

06

App Permissions

Parental Lock requests the following Android permissions, each with a specific purpose:

  • Display over other apps — required to show the lock screen overlay on top of the child's activity
  • Notifications — required to show the countdown timer notification with Pause/Resume controls
  • Foreground Service — required to keep the timer running reliably in the background
  • Receive Boot Completed — required to restart protection automatically after the phone is restarted
  • Use Biometric / Use Fingerprint — required to verify the parent's identity for unlocking
  • Read Phone State (optional) — used to hide the lock screen during incoming calls so they can always be answered
  • Schedule Exact Alarm — required to trigger the lock screen at precisely the right time
  • Device Administrator — required to prevent the child from uninstalling the app

None of these permissions are used to collect, transmit, or share data of any kind.

07

Security

Since Parental Lock stores no data on external servers, there is no server-side security risk. All data stays on the device, protected by Android's built-in security model.

  • The backup PIN is stored in Android SharedPreferences with device-level encryption
  • Biometric verification uses Android's Trusted Execution Environment (TEE)
  • The Device Administrator feature prevents unauthorized uninstallation
  • Changing the backup PIN requires biometric verification first
